== Frequently Asked Questions == 

= Where do I find my Calendar ID? = 

You can find your Calendar ID by logging into your [AddEvent account](https://app.addevent.com/calendars). Hover over your calendar name, and click the three dots. From there, click **Calendar Page**, and your Calendar ID can be found in the top calendar details section. This ID allows you to embed your Events Calendar on your WordPress site.

= How do I configure the Calendar ID? = 

After adding the AddEvent Event Calendar block, enter your Calendar ID in the settings panel to link your Events Calendar.

= Can I manage events directly in WordPress? = 

The plugin uses an external service [AddEvent] (https://www.addevent.com/?utm_source=wordpress&utm_medium=plugin&utm_campaign=embeddable-calendar&utm_term=AddEvent&utm_content=faq) for event management and is not manageable from WordPress.

= How do I uninstall the plugin? = 

Deactivate and delete the plugin from the **Plugins** menu. All related data will be removed upon deletion.
